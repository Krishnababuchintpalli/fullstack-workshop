ASUS Vivobook 16

The ASUS Vivobook 16 is a line of 16-inch laptops designed for productivity, students, and everyday users, offering a balance of performance, portability, and value. Multiple variants are available with Intel Core, AMD Ryzen, and Qualcomm Snapdragon X processors, catering to different needs including Copilot+ AI-ready computing. These laptops feature WUXGA (1920×1200) IPS displays with a 16:10 aspect ratio, 60Hz refresh rate, and up to 300 nits brightness, ideal for multitasking and media consumption. Most models come with 16GB RAM and 512GB or 1TB SSD storage, running Windows 11 with Microsoft Office pre-installed. They include modern features like fast charging (50% in 30 minutes), long battery life (up to 27 hours in some models), Wi-Fi 6/6E, backlit keyboards, fingerprint readers, and 180° lay-flat hinges for easy sharing.
